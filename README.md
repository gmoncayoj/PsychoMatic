**Automated psychometric analysis in R**

PsychoMatic is an R package that streamlines psychometric workflows using SEM-based procedures and literature-informed heuristics. It supports descriptives, EFA, CFA, reliability, measurement invariance, and alignment, with flexible settings and bilingual reporting. In addition, it provides APA 7-style references to studies that support your decisions based on the analysis you perform.

## What PsychoMatic can do?

Descriptive item analysis
Exploratory factor analysis (EFA)
Confirmatory factor analysis (CFA)
Reliability reporting
Measurement invariance
Alignment optimization
Reports in English or Spanish
Exportable outputs

PsychoMatic makes the best decisions for you so you can run your analyses with confidence!
However, you are also free to customize it according to your own specifications; in any case, it will provide you with the final report in the language of your choice (English or Spanish), ready to be submitted to your practice.

## Development status

PsychoMatic is currently in active development and is open to suggestions for improvement.
